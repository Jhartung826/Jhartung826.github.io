Humidity

Graph depicting Humidity vs Latitude

Oddly enough cities that are very close to the Latitude tend to be more humid but there is a sudden drop in humidity when dead on the equator (or close to it). The theory here is that cities become more humid as the get hotter until they hit a certain temperature. I grew up in a small desert town and while it could go up to 120 degrees F in the summer, it was never humid.
