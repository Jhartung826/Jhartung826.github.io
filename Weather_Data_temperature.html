Graph depicting Max Temperature vs Lattitude

According to the data, cities closer to the equator (lattitude of zero) tend to have higher temperatures than those farther away. However, since the data was collected during the winter solstice, cities in the sourthern hemisphere were significantly warmer than those in the norther hemisphere.
